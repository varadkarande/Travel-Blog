Goa

Beautiful beaches, nightlife, and sunsets. Goa is India's party capital with pristine beaches like Palolem and Anjuna, and a unique blend of Indian and Portuguese culture.

Manali

Snow-capped mountains and adventure sports. Nestled in the Himalayas, Manali offers breathtaking views, trekking opportunities, and the famous Rohtang Pass.

Jaipur

Historic forts, palaces, and vibrant culture. The Pink City is home to Amer Fort, Hawa Mahal, and rich Rajasthani traditions that captivate every visitor.
